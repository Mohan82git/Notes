##  Plan of action for reaching your professional, developmental or educational goals 

1. Start your plan by considering where you’d like to be and what you need to do in terms of study and training to get there.
2. Break your ultimate goal down into smaller, bite-sized chunks.
3. Investigate facilities for learning.
4. Build your study or training plan into your life with a view to keeping a balance between study, work, social and leisure activities.
5. Set a realistic timeframe to achieve your goal.

Pushing yourself to get there too fast is likely to cause you stress, impair your enjoyment of the journey to your goal, or even lead you to abandon your goal all together.
