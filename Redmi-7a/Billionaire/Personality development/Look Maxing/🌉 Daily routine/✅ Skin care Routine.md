Tips for Effective Application
Always apply serums before moisturizers.

Avoid using actives like retinol and exfoliants together.

Apply sunscreen even indoors to prevent damage from blue light.

1. **Cleanser** : Foaming cleanser with salicylic acid, ninacimide 
2. **Exfoliations** containing Beta Hydroxy acid or alpha hydroxyl acid
3. **Toner** with ninacimide or tree tea oil
4. **Vitamin** C serum
5. **Retinol**
6. **Hydroquinone**
7. **Moisturizer** without adding oil

⚠️ Avoid using Vitamin C serum with retinol or AHAs/BHAs simultaneously, as they can irritate the skin. Instead, alternate their use.
