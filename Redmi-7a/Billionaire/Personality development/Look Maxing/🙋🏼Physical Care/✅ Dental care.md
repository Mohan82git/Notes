### 1. Daily Dental Care Routine
- Brush Twice Daily
- Floss Daily
- Use Mouthwash
- Stay Hydrated
- Healthy Diet for Teeth : Avoid acidic 

### 2. Teeth Whitening Tips
1. Baking soda and hydrogen peroxide paste (once a week).
2. Oil pulling with coconut oil to remove surface stains.
3. Whitening strips (e.g., Crest 3D White).
4. LED light whitening kits.
5. Consult dental docter
