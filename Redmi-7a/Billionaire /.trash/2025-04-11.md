Here's a **homemade Maggi masala recipe** using simple Indian spices. It’s tasty, has no preservatives, and gives a similar flavor to the original:

### **Homemade Maggi Masala Powder (For 2 Noodle Packs)**

**Dry spices (roast lightly and grind if needed):**

- Coriander seeds – 1 tsp धने 
- Cumin seeds – ½ tsp
- Fennel seeds – ½ tsp 
- Black pepper – ¼ tsp
- Clove – 1
- Cinnamon – a small piece (½ inch)
- Bay leaf – ½

**Powdered spices (add as-is):**

- Turmeric powder – ¼ tsp
- Red chili powder – ½ tsp
- Dry mango powder (amchur) – ½ tsp
- Garam masala – ¼ tsp
- Dry ginger powder – ¼ tsp
- Asafoetida (hing) – a pinch
- Salt – ½ tsp (adjust to taste)
- Sugar – ½ tsp

**Optional for more flavor:**

- Onion powder – ½ tsp
- Garlic powder – ½ tsp

### **Instructions:**

1. Dry roast the whole spices (first list) on low flame for 1-2 minutes until aromatic.
2. Let them cool, then grind into a fine powder.
3. Mix in the remaining powdered spices.
4. Store in an airtight container and use 1½ to 2 tsp per noodle packet (or to your taste).
