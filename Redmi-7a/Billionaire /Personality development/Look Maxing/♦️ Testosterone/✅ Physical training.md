## 1. Physical Activities

Types of Exercises for Testosterone Boost

- ####  Resistance Training
	Focus on compound movements that recruit multiple muscle groups.
	Examples: Squats, deadlifts, bench press, pull-ups.


- ####  HIIT (High-Intensity Interval Training)
	Short, intense bursts of activity with rest intervals.
	Example: Sprint for 30 seconds, rest for 90 seconds, repeat for 15 minutes.

- ####  Flexibility and Mobility
	Yoga and dynamic stretches reduce stress and improve recovery.
	
**⚠️ Excessive training can increase cortisol and lower testosterone.**
