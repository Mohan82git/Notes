## Sample Workout Plan (4 Days a Week)


#### Day 1: Full-Body Strength Training
Squats: 3 sets x 8 reps
Deadlifts: 3 sets x 6 reps
Bench Press: 3 sets x 8 reps

#### Day 2: HIIT and Core
5 rounds of sprints (30 seconds on, 90 seconds rest).
Plank variations: 3 x 1-minute holds.

#### Day 3: Rest or Yoga
Focus on poses like Downward Dog and Child's Pose.

#### Day 4: Upper Body Strength
Pull-Ups: 3 sets x 6 reps
Overhead Press: 3 sets x 8 reps
