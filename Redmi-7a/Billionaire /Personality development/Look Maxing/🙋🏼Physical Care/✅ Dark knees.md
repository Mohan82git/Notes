Home Remedies That Work 100% of the Time


1. **Lemon and Sugar Scrub**
	- 1 Lemon
	- 2 tsp Sugar
	- 1 tsp Honey (optional)
	Frequency: Twice a week.

2. **Turmeric and Milk Paste**
	- 1 tsp Turmeric Powder
	- 2-3 tbsp Milk
	Frequency: Daily or every alternate day.

3. **Besan (Gram Flour) Pack**
	- 2 tbsp Besan (Gram Flour)
	- 1 tsp Turmeric
	- 1 tbsp Curd
	Frequency: Thrice a week.

4. **Aloe Vera and Almond Oil**
	- Fresh Aloe Vera Gel
	- 1 tsp Almond Oil
	Frequency: Daily.

5. **Potato Juice**
	- 1 Potato
	Frequency: Daily.

6. **Coconut Oil and Baking Soda Scrub**
	- 1 tbsp Coconut Oil
	- 1 tsp Baking Soda
	Frequency: Twice a week.
	
7. **Multani Mitti (Fuller's Earth) Pack**
	- 2 tbsp Multani Mitti
	- 1 tbsp Rose Water
	- A few drops of Lemon Juice
	Frequency: Thrice a week.

8. **Rice Flour Scrub**
	- 2 tbsp Rice Flour
	- 1 tbsp Curd
	Frequency: Twice a week.


Caution:

- Patch test any remedy before applying to a large area to avoid allergic reactions.
