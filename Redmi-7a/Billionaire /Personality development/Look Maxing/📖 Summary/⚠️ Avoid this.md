To improve both gut health and acne, avoid:

- **Refined** **Sugar**: Causes blood sugar spikes, feeding bad bacteria.
- **Fried Foods**: Triggers gut inflammation.
- **Dairy** (Excessive): Can worsen acne in some individuals. Opt for plant-based milk like almond or coconut milk.
- **Processed Foods:** Contain additives that harm gut flora.
- **Soy** products (excessive intake may interfere with hormone balance).
- **Alcohol** and caffeine in high quantities.
- **Fried** and trans-fat-heavy foods.
- Avoid using lemon on cuts or irritated skin, as it may cause a burning sensation.
