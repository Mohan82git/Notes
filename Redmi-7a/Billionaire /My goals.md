- #### Fight to Hair greying
	Anti-Grey Hair Herbal Pack (1–2 times a week)

- Ingredients:
	- 2 tbsp Bhringraj powder
	- 2 tbsp Amla powder 
	- 1 tbsp Brahmi powder
	- 1 tbsp Fenugreek (methi) powder
	- Water or yogurt to form a paste
	- 1 tsp black tea or coffee


- How to Use:

	1. Mix all powders in a bowl.
	2. Add water or yogurt slowly to make a smooth, thick paste.
	3. Let it sit for 15 minutes.
	4. Apply generously to your scalp and hair.
	5. Leave for 45–60 minutes.
	6. Rinse off with lukewarm water and a mild shampoo.
	
- Bonus Tip:
	 For even better results, pair this with Bhringraj oil massage twice a week and maintain a cooling, healthy diet (avoid too much spicy or fried food—these can worsen hair greying in Ayurveda).
