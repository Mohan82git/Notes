# **Roadmap for Managing Time & Meeting Deadlines in a Job (2.5 Months)**

**Goal:** Develop **time management skills**, **prioritization techniques**, and **productivity habits** to meet deadlines effectively in a professional environment.

---

## **Phase 1: Time Management Fundamentals (3 Weeks)**

### **Topics to Cover**

✔ Understanding **Time Blocking** (Allocating fixed time for tasks)  
✔ Learning **Task Prioritization** (Eisenhower Matrix, ABCDE Method)  
✔ Avoiding **Context Switching** (Focusing on one task at a time)  
✔ Setting **Realistic Goals** (SMART Goals)  
✔ Using **Pomodoro Technique** for focused work

### **Hands-on Tasks**

✅ Create a **daily schedule with time blocks for focused work**  
✅ Use the **Eisenhower Matrix** to prioritize tasks in a project  
✅ Apply the **Pomodoro Technique (25 mins work, 5 mins break)**

### **Common Mistakes to Avoid**

❌ Overestimating how much **work can be done in a day**  
❌ Not **tracking time spent** on different tasks  
❌ Multitasking too much, leading to **low-quality work**

---

## **Phase 2: Meeting Deadlines Effectively (3 Weeks)**

### **Topics to Cover**

✔ Breaking Large Tasks into **Smaller Milestones**  
✔ Creating **Realistic Work Timelines** (Avoiding Overcommitment)  
✔ Using **Project Management Tools** (Trello, Notion, Asana)  
✔ Communicating **Early About Delays** (Instead of Last-Minute Panic)  
✔ Learning **How to Say No** to Unnecessary Work

### **Hands-on Tasks**

✅ Break a **big project into smaller milestones with deadlines**  
✅ Track project progress using **Trello/Notion**  
✅ Practice **writing a professional email about a deadline extension**

### **Common Mistakes to Avoid**

❌ Not **setting buffer time** for unexpected delays  
❌ Assuming **everything will go smoothly** without problems  
❌ Avoiding **communication when falling behind**

---

## **Phase 3: Productivity Boosters & Work-Life Balance (3 Weeks)**

### **Topics to Cover**

✔ Automating **Repetitive Tasks** (Scripting, Shortcuts)  
✔ Avoiding **Burnout** (Proper Rest, Breaks, Exercise)  
✔ Setting Boundaries for **Work vs. Personal Time**  
✔ Learning **Deep Work** (Focused work without distractions)  
✔ Reflecting & Improving Time Management Every Week

### **Hands-on Tasks**

✅ Identify **3 time-consuming tasks to automate**  
✅ Set up **working hours with strict start & end times**  
✅ Spend **one work session in deep focus mode** (No distractions)

### **Common Mistakes to Avoid**

❌ Skipping breaks, leading to **mental fatigue**  
❌ Not **reviewing & adjusting time management strategies**  
❌ Saying **"yes" to every request**, causing overload

✔ **Meeting Deadlines:** Realistic planning & proactive communication
